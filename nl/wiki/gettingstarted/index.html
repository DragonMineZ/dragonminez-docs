    Getting Started: A Full Guide to play DragonMine Z

    I. Installing Minecraft Forge 1.20.1 & DragonMine Z

    Prerequisites

    Before installing a mod for Minecraft Forge 1.20.1, ensure you have the following:

    • Minecraft Java Edition (1.20.1)
    • Minecraft Forge (1.20.1)
    • A compatible mod (for Forge 1.20.1)
    • Java (latest version recommended)

    Step 1: Install Minecraft Forge

    1. Download Forge
    2. Go to the official Forge website.
    3. Select 1.20.1 from the left panel.

    4. Click on Recommended Installer to download the Forge installer.

    5. Run the Forge Installer

    6. Open the downloaded .jar file.
    7. Select Install Client and click OK.
    8. Wait for the installation to complete.
    9. Open the Minecraft Launcher and select the Forge profile.
    10. Click Play to generate the necessary Forge files.

    Step 2: Install a Mod

    1. Download DragonMine Z and GeckoLib
    2. DragonMine Z links: CurseForge or Modrinth.
    3. GeckoLib links: CurseForge or Modrinth.
    4. Open File Explorer and navigate to your Minecraft directory:
    5. Windows: C:\Users\YourUsername\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft
    6. MacOS: ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft
    7. Linux: ~/.minecraft
    8. Locate the mods folder.
      • Note: You may need to create the mods folder if it doesn't exist. Make sure to write the folder name in lowercase.
    9. Move the downloaded .jar files into the mods folder.

    Step 3: Launch Minecraft with Forge

    1. Open the Minecraft Launcher.
    2. Select the Forge installation.
    3. Increase the allocated RAM to at least 4GB.
    4. Click Play.
    5. Once the game loads, you should see the >DragonMine Z< title screen.

    II. Start your Adventure in DMZ!

    Step 1: The Basics

    1. Make sure to select the Default World Type.
    2. If your world loading gets stuck at 20% or 30%, it's completely normal. The mod is generating the structures and biomes.
    3. Once the world is loaded, you will be granted the DragonMine Z Advancement.
    4. Press V to Open the Character Creation Menu
    5. Choose your favorite race!
    6. Customize your character's appearance, colors, and fighting style.
    7. Once finished, click Confirm and you should see the new Custom GUI on the top left corner.
    8. Press V again to Open the Stats Menu
    9. Here you can see your character's stats, level, and TP.
    10. First, you have to release your power, it's represented by the 0% text in the top left corner.
    11. To release your power, you have to hold the C key, then you'll be able to see it increase up to 50%, the maximum you can reach without having the Potential Unlock skill.
    12. To obtain your first Training Points, you need to punch or kill entities! Doesn't matter if it's a cow or a zombie, just punch it!
    13. You can allocate your Training Points to increase your character's stats.
    14. Note: If you're building STR, it's recommended to have the same CON to avoid consuming all your stamina in one hit, the less stamina you have, the less damage you deal, so be careful!

    Step 2: Explore the world!

    1. Find Kami's Lookout
    2. Kami's Lookout spawns between XZ: -1200 to 1200. So it's very easy to find!
    3. Here, you can talk to Korin to get free Senzu Beans every 5 minutes, and claim a free Flying Nimbus if you don't have one!
    4. Then, you can use your brand new Nimbus to fly all the way up to Kami's Lookout!
    5. In Kami's Lookout, you can find the Dende, who can heal you for free and reset your character. Also, you can go into the Hyperbolic Time Chamber to train and get stronger, there, your TP Gain will be multiplied by 3 by default!
    6. Craft a Dragon Radar
    7. First, you have to found some Iron and Redstone, then you will be able to craft Radar Pieces, then Radar Chips, Radar CPU, and finally the Dragon Radar!
    8. With the Dragon Radar, you can find the Dragon Balls! 6 of them are scattered around the world, and the fourth one is in Goku's House!
    9. Once you have all 7 Dragon Balls, you can summon Shenron and make a wish! You can wish for Capsules that gives you a stat boost, or a Tier 2 Radar CPU. The Tier 2 Radar CPU allows you to craft the Namek Dragon Radar, so make sure to get it!
    10. Find Goku's House and Kame House
    11. If you searched the Dragon Balls, you probably found Goku's House, where you can find the 4th Dragon Ball!
    12. You can talk to Goku to learn some Skills!
    13. Kame House is located always in an Ocean Biome, so just swim around the ocean until you find it!
    14. You can talk to Master Roshi to learn some Skills too!

    Step 3: Train and make through the Storyline!

    1. Train your character
    2. You can train your character by punching or killing entities, or by using the Hyperbolic Time Chamber.
    3. Once you think you're strong enough, you can start the Storyline!
    4. To progress through the Storyline, you need to open the V menu and click on the Storyline button.
    5. Fight powerful enemies
    6. The Storyline will make you face powerful enemies, such as Raditz, Nappa, Vegeta, and more!
    7. Make sure to train and level up your character to face these challenges.
    8. Once you defeated Vegeta, you'll obtain a Saiyan Space Ship, which allows you to travel to other planets!
    9. Travel to other planets
    10. First, you'll only be able to go to Planet Namek, where you can find the Namekian Dragon Balls and Grand Elder Guru!
    11. If you haven't died yet, try it! You'll be able to explore the Otherworld and find King Kai's Planet, where you can learn the Kaioken and Potential Unlock!
    12. Then, you'll be able to travel to King Kai's Planet every time you want with the Saiyan Space Ship!
    13. Note: Make sure to revive with King Yemma before trying to travel to the Overworld again.

    Step 4: Go Beyond!

    1. Explore the mod
    2. DragonMine Z has a lot of content to explore, such as new biomes, structures, and enemies.
    3. Make sure to explore the world and find hidden secrets!
    4. Once you're ready, you can learn the SuperForms!
    5. SuperForms are the Main Transformations for every race.
    6. To learn them, you'll have to progress in the Storyline and they'll be added to the V menu.
    7. Alternatively, you can modify the config to learn make them buyable with TP.
    8. Have fun!
    9. The main goal of DragonMine Z is to have fun and enjoy the Dragon Ball universe in Minecraft.
    10. Try different playstyles, explore the world, and PvP with your friends to become the strongest warrior!
